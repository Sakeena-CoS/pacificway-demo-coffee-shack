Our Menu

Coffee the way it should be.

Sourced from a local roaster, prepared by skilled baristas, and served in a cozy atmosphere — the Coffee Shack is Westshore's daily ritual.

Espresso Drinks
Cappuccinos, lattes, flat whites, Americanos, and macchiatos — crafted with precision and locally roasted beans for a cup that's worth savouring every morning.
Cold Brew & Iced
Silky cold brew, iced lattes, and iced Americanos for the days when you need your coffee cold and refreshing. Smooth, never bitter.
Pour Over & Filter
Single-origin pour overs and drip filter coffee for those who appreciate the nuance of great coffee. Slow, deliberate, and exceptional in the cup.
Tea & Alternatives
Premium loose-leaf teas, matcha lattes, chai, and non-coffee alternatives for every member of your group — because great cafés welcome everyone.
Pastries & Snacks
Fresh-baked pastries, muffins, cookies, and grab-and-go snacks to pair perfectly with your morning coffee. Sourced locally when possible.
Seasonal Specials
Limited-edition seasonal drinks and specials that change throughout the year — reason enough to stop by and see what's new at the Shack.

Why the Shack

Westshore's local roast.

At 2778 Peatt Road in Langford, the Coffee Shack has built a loyal following in the Westshore community. We source our beans from a local roaster, employ skilled baristas who care about craft, and create an atmosphere where you genuinely want to linger.

  • Local roaster sourced beans — we support BC's coffee community and you taste the difference in every cup.
  • Skilled baristas — our team is passionate about coffee and trained to pull the perfect shot every time.
  • Cozy atmosphere — a warm, welcoming space to start your day, work remotely, or catch up with a friend.
  • Free WiFi — reliable, fast WiFi so you can work or scroll while you enjoy your coffee in peace.
  • Dog-friendly patio — bring your furry companion along and enjoy your coffee outdoors together.
