Our Story

Your neighbourhood coffee shack.

Coffee Shack Westshore started with a simple idea — that Langford deserved a proper indie coffee spot. Not a chain, not a drive-through, not another tired franchise. Just good coffee made by people who care about it.

We're small, we're deliberate, and we're on Instagram where you can see what's fresh each day. Come find us on Peatt Road.
